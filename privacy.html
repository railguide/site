Privacy Policy

Rail Guide is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the Rail Guide website and mobile apps, and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Rail Guide does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the map. When you use the site, the following may be collected automatically:

Location data: If you use the "Show location" feature, your device's GPS coordinates are used solely to center the map on your position. This data is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

Usage data: Like most websites, our hosting provider may collect standard server log information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This data is used for security and performance purposes only.

Third-Party Services

Rail Guide uses Mapbox to render maps. Mapbox may collect usage data subject to their own privacy policy. We also link to the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, which have their own privacy practices.

Cookies

Rail Guide does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Mapbox and other embedded services may set cookies as described in their respective privacy policies.

Advertising

Rail Guide does not serve third-party advertising and does not sell or share your data with advertisers.

Children's Privacy

Rail Guide does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at info@rail.guide and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@rail.guide.
